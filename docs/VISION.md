# Vision

NeoFMS is not just another MCDU simulator.

Its mission is to become the best free Airbus FMS training platform available.

## Long-term Vision

### v1

Learn to use the Airbus MCDU.

### v2

Train complete IFR flights.

### v3

AI instructor.

### v4

Shared training sessions.

### v5

Support for additional Airbus aircraft.
